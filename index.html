Learning for Everyone

Become an scientist, inventor and lifelong learner today. Sunset Spark is a not-for-profit hackerspace for the immigrant community of Sunset Park. We offer free high quality classes for kids, adults and families in a wide variety of science and engineering subjects like neuroscience, software engineering, and physical computing.

21st century skills for 21st century dreams. We work with our students to make sure the classes meet their needs and goals. All our classes incorporate hands-on projects, experiments, skills and knowledge that will immediately empower students of all ages to learn more on their own and use their new knowledge in the real world in creative and relevant ways.

Brains and Babies

Cerebros y Bebés

Study the neurological and cognitive development of babies from conception through early childhood. Are you a new or expecting mother? If so, this class is for you. We'll explore the growth of your child's brain starting from just a handful of cells all the way through to elementary school. You'll learn how babies learn, grow a better understanding of the cognitive stages of infancy and childhood, and discovered scientifically-backed ways to be a better parent.

Sign Up Now

Estudiar el desarrollo neurológico y cognitivo de bebes desde concepción hasta la primera infancia. ¿Estás esperando un bebe? ¿Eres una madre nueva? Si es así, esta clase es para ti. Exploramos el crecimiento del cerebro de su hijo/a comienzando de un solo mano de células hasta el edad de primaria. Estudiar como bebés aprenden, conoce las etapas cognitivas de nacimiento y infancia, descubre como ser mejor padre con estudios científicamente apoyado.

Robot Monster Party

Fiesta de Monstruo Robot

Build a robot monster with Lego Mindstorms. Students will hone their creativity and engineering skills by designing innovative robots using special programmable Lego kits. Kids spend the semester building robots and learning about gears, sensors, programming, and autonomous behaviors. The semester ends with a monster robot party for friends and family to check out their creations. Sorry adults, this class is kids only.

Programming for the Brain

Programación para el Cerebro

Study programming using the ultimate computer, the human brain. Have you ever wanted to know what a brainwave looks like? Teens will create EEG experiments to measure neural activity in their own brains. That might not make sense now, but it will once you take the class. This is a unique opportunity to learn computer programming and neuroscience through experimentation. Along with the new skills and knowledge, teens will leave the class with a printout of activity in their own brain.

Sign Up Now

Estudiar programación utilizando la computadora original, el cerebro humano. ¿Alguna vez has querido saber como se ve una onda cerebral? Adolescentes crean experimentos para medir actividades neuronales en sus propios cerebros. Por ahora no se entiende bien pero sería más claro cuando tomas el clase. A través de experimentación, en este oportunidad único aprendas programación de computadores y neurociencia. Junto con habilidades nuevos y conocimiento, adolescentes se llevan una copia impresa de la actividad de sus proprio cerebros.

Build a wearable computer to create colorful visualizations from your own dance moves. Take your dance moves up a notch by adding in technology. This class walks teens through the process of creating a wearable device to capture movement and motion and turn them into futuristic computer art. If you have a love for dance, this class will use your passion as an entry way into the inventive world of programming and engineering.

Sign Up Now

Si te gusta bailar, este clase utiliza su pasión como un punto de entrada de comprender el mundo inventivo de programación y ingeniería.

Science Literacy

Alfabetización Científica

Help your little one learn about science through hands on experiments. The best kind of fair is a science fair. Every week parents and kids (ages 4-6) team up to carry out a fun science experiment using household materials. Experiments might get a little messy. This class is beginner friendly and helps build an English vocabulary around scientific words and concepts. It's never too early or too late to become a scientist.

Sign Up Now

Ayuda tu pequeño aprender ciencias a través de experimentos interactivos. El mejor tipo de ferias es una feria de ciencias. Cada semana padres y niños (edades 4-6) trabajan juntos para realizar experimentos de ciencias divertidos usando enseres domésticos. Puede ensuciarse en los experimentos. Esta clase es bueno para iniciadores y ayuda desarrollar el vocabulario científico con palabras y conceptos en inglés. Nunca es demasiado tarde para ser un científico.
