Learning for Everyone

Become an engineer, scientist, inventor and lifelong learner today. Sunset Spark is a not-for-profit hackerspace for the immigrant community of Sunset Park. We offer free classes for kids and adults in a wide variety of science and engineering subjects like neuroscience, software engineering, and physical computing.

We work with our students to make sure the classes meet their needs. The best way to learn is to be engaged. All our classes have hands-on projects, experiments or immediate takeaways that will empower students of all ages to learn more on their own and use their new knowledge in the real world in creative and relevant ways.

Our classes take place in borrow spaces like schools, parks and offices around Sunset Park. We don't have a permanent home to call our own yet, but we're working toward opening a permanent location in the neighborhood soon to open up a technology workshop and offer more science and engineering classes to the community.
